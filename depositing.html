Depositing into Deep Blue Documents

How to Deposit

The process for depositing a Work into Deep Blue Documents is outlined below. This process is different for U-M faculty/staff compared to students. To determine eligibility, please refer to the "Eligibility" section on the About page. If you are a student, please consult the "Student Deposit Process" on the About page for further guidance. For U-M faculty and staff, please follow the steps below:

  1. Log in to Deep Blue Documents
    Navigate to Deep Blue Documents and click "Login" in the upper right-hand corner.
  2. Start a New Deposit
    Open your Deep Blue Documents dashboard by clicking on the menu icon in the upper left-hand corner. Click “Deposit” under "+ New” to begin a new deposit.
  3. Select a Collection
    A pop-up will appear prompting you to select a collection. If a collection appears, select it and proceed. If not, contact your collection administrator to request access.
  4. New Deposit Page
    After selecting a collection, you will be directed to the New Deposit page. Automate metadata such as Deposit Date, Depositor's Name, and more will be assigned to your Work.
  5. Provide Required Metadata
    Fill in required details like Depositor Information, Author names, and Upload Files. Select a Creative Commons License and review the Deposit License.
  6. Check for Completion
    A green check mark will appear next to each section once all required information is entered.
  7. Finalizing Your Deposit
    Use Discard, Save, Save for later, or Deposit options to finalize. Note: Depositing locks the item and its metadata.

Missing Collections While Depositing

If you can’t find a collection in the dropdown box, you haven’t been added as a depositor. Please contact your collection administrator. If you have additional questions, feel free to Contact Us.

Automatic and Enhanced Metadata

Deep Blue Documents automatically assigns several pieces of metadata at the time of deposit. For improvements in access, the U-M Library may enhance the metadata.

Additional Information on Depositing Works

Accepted File Formats

All digital formats are accepted, but preservation support varies. Research data should be deposited into Deep Blue Data.

File Size Limitations

Files larger than 5 GB require staff assistance for depositing. Please Contact Us for help with large files.

Prohibited Types of Works

Examples include data sets, software, administrative records, and works in progress. Contact Us for guidance.

Inserting Special Characters

Special characters can be included using system-specific methods described for Mac and Windows users.

Batch Upload Process

If you have more than twenty documents, staff can assist with a batch upload. Contact Us for more information.
